Matrix Transformations in $\mathbb{R}^2$

The goal of this applet is to help you explore how a matrix transformation affects vectors in two dimensions. You can modify the matrix A by adjusting the sliders. To see the how the matrix affects the vector, you can drag $\textcolor{blue}{\mathbf{x}}$ around the coordinate plane. The image vector $\textcolor{red}{A\mathbf{x}}$ will adjust accordingly.
